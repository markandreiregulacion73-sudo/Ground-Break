About Us

Welcome to Ground Break Billiards Cafe — a place where fun, relaxation, and unforgettable memories come together.

Founded on October 31, 2024 by Froilan Garcia and Shine Basillio, Ground Break was created to bring people together in a warm, welcoming, and family-friendly environment.

More than just a billiard cafe, it is a space where customers can relax, socialize, and enjoy quality time with friends and loved ones.

Our cafe is designed to create positive experiences where people from different walks of life can connect, build friendships, and create lasting memories.

We value customer satisfaction by providing quality service, a clean and comfortable atmosphere, and an enjoyable place for entertainment and bonding.

Whether customers visit to unwind after school or work, enjoy good food, sing karaoke, or simply spend time with others, Ground Break aims to make every visit meaningful and enjoyable.

Through the continuous support of our community and loyal customers, we remain committed to improving our services, expanding our reach, and becoming a trusted destination for relaxation, recreation, and family-friendly entertainment in the local community.
